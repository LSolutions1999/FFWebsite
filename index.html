About

Handmade pieces with a personal finish

Faceted Frames creates sculptural jewelry that feels collected, not mass-produced. Every pendant and set is built by hand, with an emphasis on texture, balance, and the natural beauty of each stone.

The aesthetic leans rich and timeless: dark green surfaces, warm gold accents, and a crafted look that makes the jewelry feel like a keepsake from the moment it is worn.
